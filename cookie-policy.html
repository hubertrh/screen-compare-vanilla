Cookie Policy for ScreenCompare

Hi there!

We want you to have the best experience on our site, and that's why we use cookies. Don't worry, they're just small files that get saved to your computer and make your time here even better. Here's a little more information about what cookies do, why we use them, and how you can control them.

Why We Love Cookies

Cookies help us make our site better for you! They let us remember your preferences and give us insight into how you use our site. We use this information to make sure you have the best experience possible.

Don't Worry, You're in Control

If you're not sure about cookies, don't worry, you can still use our site. But, if you prefer, you can adjust your browser settings to stop cookies from being saved. Just keep in mind that doing so may affect some of the features on our site.

Our Cookies

We have two types of cookies: site preferences and third-party cookies.

  • Site preferences cookies help us remember your preferences and give you a more personalised experience.
  • Third-party cookies, like Google Analytics and such, help us understand how you use our site and give us ideas for ways to make it even better. These cookies may track things such as how long you spend on the site and the pages that you visit so we can continue to produce engaging content.

More Information

Hopefully that has clarified things for you and as was previously mentioned if there is something that you aren't sure whether you need or not it's usually safer to leave cookies enabled in case it does interact with one of the features you use on our site.
